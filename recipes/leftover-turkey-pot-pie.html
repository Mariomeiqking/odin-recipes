Leftover Turkey Pot Pie

Description

Put your Thanksgiving leftovers to good use with this turkey pot pie recipe. The Allrecipes community can't get enough of the top-rated recipe.

One reviewer says it has become a "post-holiday tradition!"

Ingredients

  • Frozen veggies
  • Fresh veggies
  • Butter and Flour
  • Seasonings
  • Broth
  • Milk
  • Turkey
  • Pie Crust

Steps

  1. Cook the frozen vegetables and celery.
  2. Saute the onions in butter, then whisk in the flour and seasonings.
  3. Whisk in the broth and milk to thicken the sauce.
  4. Stir in the cooked vegetables and turkey.
  5. Pour the filling into the prepared pie crust.
  6. Cover with the remaining crust and bake.
